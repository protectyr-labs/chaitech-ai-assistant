# Curated picks from the Claude community

Not every useful helper was made by someone in our cohort. This page points to the best ones built elsewhere. They are linked, not copied. You install them from the original source, and the original author keeps credit for their work.

## How a helper lands on this page

1. A cohort member or steward has actually used it in their own work.
2. The author is still maintaining it (not an abandoned side project).
3. The author's license allows others to use and install it.

That is the bar. It is deliberately small.

## Current picks

### For research

**[claude-research-skill](https://github.com/altmbr/claude-research-skill) by Bryan Altman**

Turns Claude into a tiny research team. You ask a research question, it splits the question into smaller parts, sends each part to a different researcher, then brings everything back as a synthesized answer.

Good for: market research, competitive analysis, due diligence, anything where you want more than a single ChatGPT search.

*Bryan taught a session to our cohort on 2026-04-21 and this was his recommended skill.*
